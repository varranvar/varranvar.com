a logical constructed language

  • The language:
    • is a logical conlang
    • is designed for writing descriptions in quantified self logs
    • is useful because it is:
      • formally analyzable, which natural languages are not
      • expressive, which many logging tools are not
      • pleasant to write with, which many logical languages are not
    • features two operations:
      • application, where words separated by white space form a relationship
      • conjunction, where if words separated by whitespace are placed in brackets, the external statement is applied with every entry in the list
    • is demonstrated with these examples:
n-lang English
(wolf) A wolf.
(big wolf) A big wolf.
(big (bad wolf)) A wolf that is bad in a big way.
([big bad] wolf) A big bad wolf.
(from (look I wolf) (the window)) I look at the wolf from the window.
