Patience, a frictionless note-taking tool

  • Patience:
    • is an experimental personal knowledge management tool with two defining features:
      1. Nameless notes: Notes are created with a unique identifiers instead of having to be manually named
      2. Transclusion: where links to content are replaced with the content itself
    • is based on the Zettelkasten method
    • is intended as an experiment to find the advantages and disadvantages of this type of note-taking
    • is expected to provide a smoother experience for writing notes but a worse experience for reading notes
